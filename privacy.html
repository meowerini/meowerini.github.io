Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

This is a personal photo gallery. It collects almost nothing, and it never sells or advertises. This page explains exactly what happens with the little data it does touch.

Who runs this site

This gallery is a personal, non-commercial project run by an individual (the “owner”). It is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. There is no company, no ad network, and no analytics or tracking service embedded in the site.

What we collect

You can browse the entire gallery without giving up any personal information. Data is only collected if you choose to log in and vote on photos.

If you sign in with Discord, our authentication provider (Supabase) stores the basic profile that Discord shares with us:

  • your Discord user ID,
  • your username / display name,
  • your avatar image URL,
  • and the email address associated with your Discord account.

If you vote on a photo, we store a single row linking your account ID to that photo’s ID (user_id + photo_id). That’s how the site knows which photos you’ve voted for and shows public vote totals. Remove your vote and the row is deleted.

We do not collect analytics, use advertising cookies, run fingerprinting, or build any profile of your browsing.

How your login is stored on your device

When you sign in, Supabase keeps a session token in your browser’s local storage so you stay logged in between visits. This is functional, not tracking — signing out or clearing your browser storage removes it.

Third parties that make the site work

Because the site is static, a few external services handle specific jobs. When your browser talks to them, they necessarily see your IP address and request details, governed by their own privacy policies:

  • GitHub Pages — hosts and serves the website (privacy statement).
  • Supabase — handles Discord login and stores votes (privacy policy).
  • Discord — the identity provider you authenticate with (privacy policy).
  • Google Fonts — serves the site’s typefaces (privacy policy).
  • The image CDN (e.g. Cloudflare R2 or jsDelivr) — serves the photos themselves.

How we use the data

The information above is used for one purpose only: to let you log in and to record and display photo votes. It is never sold, rented, or shared for marketing, and it isn’t used for any purpose beyond running these features.

Data retention

Your account and votes are kept for as long as your account exists. Delete a vote and its record is removed immediately. Ask for your account to be deleted (see below) and your profile and all associated votes are removed.

Your choices and rights

  • Browse freely without logging in at all.
  • Sign out at any time to end your session on this device.
  • Remove any vote to delete that record.
  • Request access to, or deletion of, the data tied to your account by contacting the owner.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and Discord itself requires users to meet a minimum age. Please don’t use the login feature if you’re under that age.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “last updated” date above will change with it. Continued use of the site after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions, or a request about your data? Reach out to the site owner via the contact method listed on the gallery’s home page or its GitHub repository.
